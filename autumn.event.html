Autumn Fair 2021

Dear International Officers,

We, the Azeri Student Team, take immense pleasure in inviting you to attend the Azeri Student Autumn Fair 2021.

Our Educational Fairs have proven themselves to not only be the most popular in the market being abundantly attended by high quality, legitimately prospective students but also growing in productivity for our partners on a regular basis.

Our Fairs will be held on the following dates:

October 2: Kiev

October 3: Lviv

October 9: Tbilisi

October 10: Baku

October 17: Tirana

The Azerbaijan Government and SOCAR oil company have issued scholarships for local students who are willing to study at TOP 500 universities in the world. This is a golden opportunity to travel to Azerbaijan and promote your institution amongst the students.

Registration for Fairs will be till the first week of September. We are more than happy to offer early bird discounts and attendance at all fairs discount till July 7th. The Price for participation in the Baku fair will be 1200 pounds and for the Tbilisi fair 900 pounds.

Looking forward to seeing you all in person again and recruiting qualified and enthusiastic international students for you.

In the short-term, if you have any questions or need further information, please do not hesitate to contact us.
