About

We, Azerbaijani’s leading educational counseling agency, were founded in 2008. Since 2008, we’ve had more than 5.000 students whom we place at the universities (foundation, undergraduate and graduate), language schools, high schools and colleges. Our students studied in various countries such as United States of America, Canada, United Kingdom, Australia, Malta, New Zealand, Spain, Italy, Germany, Holland, Switzerland, China and France.

We’ve been working with prestigious institutions all around the world such as St. Andrews University, Brunel University London, Reading University, Lancaster University, Leicester University, Aberdeen University, Robert Gordon University, University of Surrey, Anglia Ruskin University, Royal Holloway University London, Exeter University, Aberystwyth University, Glion London, Les Roches institute of Hotel Management , EU business school, Glasgow University, Cambridge Education Group, Study Group, Navitas, Shorelight group universities … and more!

We’ve opened our 4th office. Now, Azeri Student has offices not only in Baku, but also in Ukraine, Georgia and Albania.

We’re running educational fairs in Azerbaijan, Ukraine and Georgia 2 times in a year.

We’re the founder of the future of education counseling service, Azeri Student, which is the most unique educational web site (www.azeristudent.az)

We’ve had our own language schools in Azerbaijan and Educational Magazine.
